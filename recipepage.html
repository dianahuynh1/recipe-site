Recipe

Ingredients

  • 3/4 C shortening
  • 2 1/2 C sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 3 TB cocoa
  • 3 TB red food coloring
  • 3 C cake flour
  • 1/2 TS salt
  • 1 1/2 C buttermilk
  • 1 1/3 TS vanilla
  • 1 1/2 TB vinegar
  • 1 1/2 TS baking soda
  • 3-8oz blocks of cream cheese
  • 1 stick of butter, softened
  • 1/2 TS vanilla
  • 16 oz powdered sugar

    • Directions

    Preheat oven to 350*.

    Grease and flour cake pan(s). Cream shortening and sugar. Add eggs. Slowly add cocoa and food coloring. Sift together cake flour and salt. Add flour mixture alternately with buttermilk beginning and ending with flour. Add in vanilla and beat for 2 minutes. Mix baking soda and vinegar together. Add and stir until combined. Bake for 30 minutes.

    Cream butter and cream cheese. Add in vanilla. Gradually stir in powdered sugar until the frosting reaches desired consistency.
